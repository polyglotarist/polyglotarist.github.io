About Me

I'm a Full-Stack Developer currently working at Infosys, where I design and build enterprise software solutions. I hold a B.S. in Computer Science from San Francisco State University (2023) with a minor in Mathematics.

My professional journey spans the full stack: C# / ASP.NET Core and Java / Spring Boot on the backend, Vue.js and React on the frontend, and relational databases including SQL Server and PostgreSQL. I've shipped REST APIs, containerized services with Docker, and collaborated in Agile teams at Revature and Infosys.

I'm also fluent in five languages — Arabic, French, English, Spanish, and Italian — and bring experience from the hospitality industry where I honed communication and teamwork. Outside work, chess keeps my problem-solving sharp.
